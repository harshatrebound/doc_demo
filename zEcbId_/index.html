Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and appointment process.

You can schedule an appointment in several ways: by clicking the "Book Appointment" button on our website, calling our office during business hours at (123) 456-7890, or emailing us at appointments@sportsorthopedics.example. Our staff will assist you in finding a convenient date and time.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our office to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan, as coverage may vary.

Please bring your insurance card, a valid photo ID, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records or imaging studies (X-rays, MRIs), and a referral from your primary care physician if required by your insurance. Also, arrive 15 minutes early to complete initial paperwork.

We treat a wide range of orthopedic conditions, including sports injuries, arthritis, fractures, ligament and tendon injuries, joint pain, and degenerative conditions. Our specialists focus on specific areas such as knee, shoulder, hip, ankle, elbow, and wrist conditions.
